# 🎓 AI Student Impact Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The AI Student Impact Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project that analyzes the impact of Generative AI on students' academic performance, study habits, skill retention, AI dependence, and mental well-being. The dashboard provides data-driven insights into how students across different majors and academic years use AI tools and how these factors influence learning outcomes.

![AI Student Impact Analytics Dashboard](https://github.com/itz-rakhi/AI_Student_Impact/blob/main/Screenshot%202026-06-27%20224406.png)
--

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze the effect of AI on students' GPA.
- Understand AI usage patterns across academic years.
- Measure AI dependence and skill retention.
- Evaluate burnout and anxiety levels.
- Compare AI adoption across majors.
- Examine institutional AI policies.
- Provide interactive filtering for deeper analysis.

## 📈 Key Insights

- Students' average GPA increased after adopting AI tools.
- AI is primarily used for debugging, writing, and brainstorming.
- STEM students demonstrate the highest academic performance.
- Higher study hours are generally associated with higher GPAs.
- Burnout increases alongside higher anxiety levels.
- Skill retention remains high despite AI adoption.
- Most institutions allow AI usage with proper citation.
- Paid AI subscriptions are more common than free versions.
